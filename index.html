    SOMNIASCULLERY PRO TIPS

    Welcome to your highly aesthetic, offline-first digital kitchen. Here is how to make the most of it:

    • WakeLock Active: SomniaScullery uses the WakeLock API. Your screen will NOT go to sleep while the app is visible—perfect for when your hands are covered in batter!
    • Drag & Drop Planning: In the Meal Plan tab, drag recipes from the sidebar directly onto the calendar days.
    • Dietary Tags: Tag your recipes (e.g., Keto, RPG, GF). Click the tags in the cookbook to filter.
    • Local First: Everything—images, recipes, meal plans—lives exclusively in your device's IndexedDB. Export your Scullery data in the AI/Settings tab to back it up!

    🤖 Pro Tip: Awakening the AI Sous Chef

    The AI Sous Chef can auto-generate recipes or full meal plans based on your specific macros, budgets, and family preferences.
    1. Go to aistudio.google.com and sign in with your Google account.
    2. Click "Get API key" and create a key.
    3. Open The Scullery -> AI Sous Chef tab.
    4. Paste your key and click Save Key.
    Example Prompt: "I need an 1800 calorie, 150g protein daily meal plan for a family of 4 on a budget. Give me recipes."
